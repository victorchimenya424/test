Services

1.Towing to a nearest garage or home

When your vehicle suddenly breaks down, you shouldn’t have to worry about how you’ll get it to safety. Our towing service is available to pick up your car from any location and transport it carefully to the nearest trusted garage — or straight to your home if you prefer. We use modern tow trucks, trained drivers, and safe loading techniques to protect your vehicle from scratches, dents, or damage. Whether it’s day or night, short distance or long distance, our goal is to make sure you are not left stranded and your car gets exactly where it needs to go, smoothly and stress-free.

2.Recovery in Time of Car Accidents

Car accidents are unexpected and overwhelming. In moments like these, you need support — not more stress. Our accident recovery team responds quickly, ensuring your vehicle is safely removed from the road while protecting both you and other drivers.We take care of the entire process: securing the vehicle, loading it correctly, and transporting it to a repair shop, insurance-approved yard, or any location of your choice. Our staff is trained to work calmly and professionally, helping you stay safe and giving you guidance on the next steps when everything feels chaotic.

3.Car Transportation

Sometimes you don’t need a tow — you simply need your car moved safely from one place to another. Whether you’re relocating, buying or selling a vehicle, or transporting a car for repairs, we provide reliable and secure vehicle transportation.Your car is handled like our own. We plan the route, load it correctly, monitor the journey, and make sure it arrives on time and in perfect condition. This service is ideal for private vehicles, company cars, and even light trucks.

4.Flat Tire Change

A flat tire can happen anywhere — at work, on the highway, or even right outside your home. Instead of struggling with tools or risking your safety on the roadside, our team will come to you and change the tire quickly and correctly.We safely lift the car, replace the flat tire with your spare (or assist in finding a repair), and make sure everything is tightened and secure before you continue your trip. It’s fast, convenient, and designed to keep you safe while saving you time

5.Electric Battery Jump Start

A dead battery doesn’t have to ruin your day. If your car refuses to start, our technicians can arrive with the right equipment to safely jump-start your vehicle. We check connections, protect your electrical system, and get you back on the road in minutes. If your battery is completely worn out, we can also advise you on replacement options or help connect you with a reliable supplier — so you’re not left stranded again.

5.Basic Mechanical Tasks (Including Engine Oil Change)

Keeping your car in good condition doesn’t always require a full workshop visit. We handle simple mechanical jobs that keep your vehicle running smoothly and help prevent breakdowns.This includes engine oil changes, basic fluid checks, minor adjustments, and routine inspections. These small services extend the life of your engine, improve performance, and reduce the risk of expensive repairs later.
